Our Services

Comprehensive real estate solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of modern Kenyan homeowners and investors.

01 - Buying Property add

Buying property is one of life's most significant milestones, whether you're purchasing your first home, expanding your investment portfolio, or securing a piece of land. We make the journey simple, secure, and rewarding. From verified listings and coordinated site visits to strategic negotiations and seamless ownership transfers, we handle the heavy lifting so you don't have to. The Kianyingi Edge: Every great investment begins with trust. We prioritize rigorous due diligence and transparent advice, so you buy with total peace of mind.

02 - Selling Property remove

Your property deserves the right market exposure, the right audience, and the optimal price. We combine targeted digital marketing, premium property presentations, and our extensive client network to connect your listing with serious, qualified buyers. From strategic pricing to final closing, we manage the entire sales journey for a smooth, successful transaction. Our Promise to Sellers: We focus on maximizing your asset's value while keeping you informed, supported, and confident at every stage.

03 - Property Sourcing add

Finding the ideal property can be time-consuming, that's where we excel. We proactively source high-quality, fully vetted properties that align with your budget, lifestyle, and financial goals, whether you're after residential, commercial, or agricultural land. Value Beyond the Search: We don't just find listings, we uncover opportunities that build long-term wealth.

04 - Leasing add

We bridge the gap between landlords seeking reliable asset management and tenants searching for a place to call home. From property marketing and strict tenant screening to managed viewings and legal lease agreements, we streamline the entire leasing process for both parties. Our Approach: We build lasting partnerships rooted in professionalism and mutual benefit, making leasing entirely hassle-free.
